Seven Springs Winter Retreat

Join us for our 9th Annual Winter Youth retreat! With the blessing of H.G. Bishop Karas, the retreat will be open to the whole diocese (DE, PA, MD, WV). The retreat is hosted by Fr. Bishoy, Fr. Daniel, and St. Mary's Coptic Orthodox Church in Pittsburgh, PA.

Theme: Where am I? ("Where are you, Adam"? Gen 3:9)
For whom: Middle School, High School, and College. Middle Schoolers must be 13 years of age or older by Mar. 8 to register.
Location: Seven Springs Resort, Champion PA

Our guest speaker for the Middle & High School group will be Fr. Daniel Nakhla of St. Mary's in Pittsburgh, and our guest speaker for the college group will be Fr. David Milad of St. Barnabas & St. Susanna in Baltimore.

The cost is $125 per person for lodging, outdoor activities, & food. Transportation and chaperones for middle/high school should be organized by each church separately.

The deadline to register & pay is Sunday, Feb 10, 2019. Space is limited, so it is to your advantage to register as early as possible! Please pray for a spiritually fruitful retreat, and God willing, we will see you there.
