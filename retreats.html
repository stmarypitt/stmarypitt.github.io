Jumonville Summer Retreat

Join us for our annual Jumonville summer retreat! Youth in the 4th - 12th grades are welcome to sign up and attend.

We will leave church on Sunday, July 28 at 2 PM (after Sunday school), and spend two nights at Jumonville. We will return to church on Tuesday, July 30th, at 3 PM.

Cost: 100$ before June 30th, $115 before July 7th. Registration will close July 7th. No exceptions.

Please register here.

You can pay when you register above, or by making a check payable to St. Mary's Coptic Orthodox / paying with cash and giving it to Sally Henein or Morcos Habib.

The Logos Retreat

The Logos Retreat is an annual retreat (usually taking place in May) for college youth and young professionals, which generally features H.G. Bishop Angelos as a guest speaker. Check back around April 2018 to register for next year's retreat! In the mean time, enjoy His Grace's sermons from previous years.
