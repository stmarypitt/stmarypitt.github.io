Annual Trip to Soergel Orchards

The day will begin with a divine liturgy at 9:00 am. We will eat a quick lunch and then head to Soergel Orchards after its conclusion. Any children attending elementary school or below will need to be supervised by a parent, and there will be a sleepover at church later in the evening for middle and high school students. There will also be pumpkin carving in the church before Vespers.
