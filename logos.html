Logos Retreat

The Logos Retreat is an annual retreat (usually taking place in May) for college youth and young professionals, which generally features H.G. Bishop Angelos as a guest speaker. Check back around January 2017 to register for next year's retreat! In the mean time, enjoy His Grace's sermons from previous years.
